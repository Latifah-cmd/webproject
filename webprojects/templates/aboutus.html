About Tifah's Closet

Mission:

Our mission is to create an apparel company
that can offer superior design,
quality and value to the consumer.
We will accomplish this by being committed
to offering great service and real value
to our business partners and consumers.

Values:

values include ethical sourcing and manufacturing,
environmental consciousness, products built to last,
diversity and inclusion, operational transparency,
and giving back to the community.
